Honey Jerk Wings

Sweet, spicy, Jamaican jerk goodness. A BBQ favourite.

Sweetness counteracts the hot kick of jerk spices in these sticky jerk chicken wings - brilliant finger food!

Cook on the BBQ over coals with a bit of cherry-wood smoke for the ultimate flavour!

Ingredients

For the wings

  • 2-3kg chicken wings
  • 3 heaped teaspoons of wet jerk seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons smoke paprika
  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt

For the honey jerk sauce

  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon wet jerk seasoning
  • 2 limes, juiced

Garnish

  • Handful coriander, chopped finely
  • Handful mint, chopped finely

Method

Cooking the wings

  1. In a large dish, toss the chicken wings with the salt, smoked paprika and chilli powder
  2. Next, add the wet jerk seasoning to the wings
  3. Leave to marinate for at least 2 hours, ideally overnight
  4. Cook over hot coals for 12-15 minutes, until crispy and completely cooked through

Adding the honey-jerk sauce

  1. For the honey-jerk sauce, combine the honey, wet jerk seasoning, and lime juice
  2. Toss the cooked wings in the sauce, coating well
  3. Optional step: Return the wings to hot coals for 1-2 minutes to caramelise the coated wings
  4. Garnish with chopped mint and coriander!
