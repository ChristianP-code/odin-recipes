Honey Jerk Wings

Sweet, spicy, Jamaican jerk goodness. A BBQ favourite.

Sweetness counteracts the hot kick of jerk spices in these sticky jerk chicken wings - brilliant finger food!

Cook on the BBQ over coals with a bit of cherry-wood smoke for the ultimate flavour!

Ingredients

For the wings

  • 2-3kg chicken wings
  • 3 heaped teaspoons of wet jerk seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons smoke paprika
  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt

For the honey jerk sauce

  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon wet jerk seasoning
  • 2 limes, juiced

Garnish

  • Handful coriander, chopped finely
  • Handful mint, chopped finely
